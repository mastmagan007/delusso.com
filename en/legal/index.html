    Legal

    DeLussol Management is committed to protecting your privacy on this website. Please read this privacy statement and cookies carefully to understand how we collect and use your personal data.

    General

    This privacy and cookie statement applies to all personal information collected and used by the DeLussol Mangement entity responsible for the relevant part of the website. The Privacy and Cookie Statement is based on DeLussol Management’s (BCR) Binding Corporate Rules for Customer, Supplier and Business Partner Data. The DeLussol Mangement entity responsible for the relevant part of the website is hereinafter referred to as “DeLussol Management”.
    DeLussol Management respects your privacy and complies with the provisions of EU legislation on the protection of privacy and, where applicable, local legislation on privacy.

    Cookies

    We only store “cookies” on your computer when you have given us your consent by clicking on the appropriate button(s) during your visit to our website. Cookies are small text files that your browser stores locally on your computer. Cookies enable us to recognise your computer the next time you visit us. In doing so, DeLussol Management is able to improve the user-friendliness of the website and to adapt the website to your needs. Most Internet browsers are configured to accept these cookies automatically.

    The type of personal information that we collect as a result of a cookie is specific to your computer and may include the Internet Protocol (IP) address, the date and time of access to our website and the parts of the website that are visited. We collect the personal information mentioned above to monitor the use and usefulness of our website, for statistical purposes and to send you information about our company and our (new) products and services that match your interests.
    You can configure your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to refuse to receive it. You can also withdraw your consent at any time by re-accessing our website and selecting the correct options. By changing the settings, you can also determine that your browser will not accept cookies from this website. Please be aware that in this case you may not be able to use all the possibilities and functionalities of our website.

    Google Analytics

    We currently use Google Analytics to analyse the website’s audience and improve our content. No personal information is collected from Google Analytics. Click here for more information about the privacy policy concerning Google Analytics.

    Data collection, purpose and recipients

    We collect personal data when you provide it to us (for example, name, address, zip code, city, telephone number and e-mail address), when you register, fill out forms or send e-mails, when you order products or services, make requests or inquiries, or in other situations in which you choose to provide us with personal information.
    Your data will be processed for the purpose of fulfilling any agreement between you and DeLussol Management, to respond to a request for information or advice from you, to meet legal or other requirements, to provide an optimal service and to send you information about our company and our (new) products or services that are of interest to you.

    Do we disclose your information to third parties?

    DeLussol Management may use third parties. Insofar as these third parties process your data in the context of the provision of the services concerned, they do so in their capacity as subcontractors for DeLussol Management, and DeLussol Management has taken the necessary technical and organisational measures to ensure that your data is processed exclusively for the aforementioned purposes.

    How do we secure your data?

    DeLussol Management treats your personal data with the utmost care. We have taken various technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against loss or misuse. Thus, we secure our systems according to the applicable security controls of the information security standards.

    Jurisdiction and applicable law

    The use of the site castell-management.com is governed by Belgian law. The courts of the district of Brussels-Capital have exclusive jurisdiction to hear any disputes that may arise from these general terms and conditions.
